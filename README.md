ISP/SPI header pinout
=====================
Note that this is NOT standard ICSP!

|pin|function
| 1 | SCK
| 2 | GND
| 3 | MISO
| 4 | VCC
| 5 | (NC)
| 6 | RST
| 7 | (NC)
| 8 | (NC)
| 9 | MOSI
|10 | GND

Microcontroller connections
===========================

Red LED on PB6

## buttons ##
PC2 - Down
PC3 - Up
PC0 - Select
PC1 - Enter

## serial header ##
PD1 - I/O R
PD0 - I/O T
I/O X is NC
